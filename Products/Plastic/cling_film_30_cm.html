Cling Film 30 cm

SKU: DFAC210009
  • PCS/PACK 6
  • UOM Carton
  • Carton weight 7.5 kg
  • Carton Size 33.5*25.8*18 cm
  • CBM 0.0156
  • Description

The Cling Film (30 cm) provides a convenient and effective way to preserve food freshness and prevent leaks. With its compact width, it's suitable for wrapping smaller items or covering plates and bowls. Whether storing leftovers in the refrigerator, protecting food during transport, or reheating dishes in the microwave, this cling film offers a secure seal and easy handling, ensuring your food stays fresh and flavorful whenever you need it.
